Apple is a technology company headquartered in Cupertino, California. It was founded by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne in 1976. 

Tim Cook is the current CEO of Apple. He succeeded Steve Jobs in 2011. 

Apple created the iPhone, which revolutionized the smartphone industry. The company also makes MacBooks, iPads, and the Apple Watch.

Microsoft is another major technology company. It was founded by Bill Gates and Paul Allen in 1975. Satya Nadella is the current CEO of Microsoft.

Google is a technology company founded by Larry Page and Sergey Brin in 1998. Sundar Pichai is the CEO of Google.
